AFGHAN WOMEN, PEACE AND SECURITY

International Peace Day

On 21 September 2025, Hope in Action held an impactful online conference, “Afghan Women, Peace & Security”, to mark International Peace Day. Moderated by Sonia Belmihoub (HR Manager, Hope in Action) and Anzhelika Semenchuk (Communication Officer, Hope in Action), the event brought together leading voices on gender justice and peacebuilding.

Our distinguished speakers

-Khaled Ahmad Zekriya (Ambassador of Afghanistan in Italy)

-Wali Rahman Rahmani (Research Scholar & Policy Advocate on Education and Geopolitics)

-Negina Yari (Education & Peace Expert and Activist)

Discussed the urgent security challenges Afghan women face under the current regime, including restrictions on education, work, movement, and participation in peace processes. The conference amplified the voices of Afghan women and highlighted pathways for global solidarity and advocacy to protect their rights and security.
